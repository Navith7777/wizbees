WizBees® Apiary (Portal)

WizBees® Apairy, an integrated platform to managne the WizBees® Hive & WizBees® Workers.

WizBees® Apairy is enabled with interactive dashboards to monitor in real-time.

WizBees®Apairy Portal allows you to configure or upgrade the configurations of WizBees® Hive & WizBees® Workers.

WizBees® Hive (Gateway)

WizBees® Hive are built with active-active or active-standby modes for multi-mode communication.

WizBees® Hive has in-built storage and enabled with store & forward. mechanism to prevent data loss

WizBees® Hive works as a standalone gateway or in networks enabled with bi-directional. communication to WizBees® Apairy.

WizBees® Worker (End-point or Sensors)

WizBees® Worker,the end-point device with built-in sensor or array of sensors, which can communicate through WizBees® Hive or WizBees® Apairy.

WizBees®Worker are the end-point device with built-in sensor or array of sensors.

WizBees®Worker has wide range of sensor & communication configurations.
